About Us

Welcome to Evergreen Institute of Engineering & Technology (EIET), where innovation meets education. Established with the vision of empowering students to become future leaders in the field of engineering and technology, EIET stands as a beacon of academic excellence.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution, shaping skilled engineers who lead in innovation, sustainability, and technology.

Our Mission

  • To offer world-class education that is aligned with industry needs.
  • To provide hands-on learning experiences through state-of-the-art labs and industry collaborations.
  • To foster creativity, critical thinking, and leadership qualities among students.

Why Choose Us?

  • Innovative Curriculum: Industry-oriented programs that keep pace with evolving technologies.
  • Expert Faculty: Learn from experienced educators with both academic and industry experience.
  • State-of-the-Art Infrastructure: Well-equipped labs, modern classrooms, and digital learning tools.
  • Placement Opportunities: Strong industry connections ensuring excellent career placement prospects.
  • Holistic Growth: Focus on extracurricular activities that develop both technical skills and personal growth.
